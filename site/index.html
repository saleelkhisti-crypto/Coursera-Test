Jurrasic Park Background Guide

Jurassic Park, the landmark 1993 science fiction adventure film directed by Steven Spielberg, takes place on the fictional island of Isla Nublar off the coast of Costa Rica. The plot follows wealthy industrialist John Hammond, who has used advanced genetic engineering to clone de-extinct dinosaurs from ancient DNA preserved in amber. After a tragic accident results in the death of a dinosaur handler, Hammond's nervous investors demand an safety inspection of the park. To secure authorization, Hammond invites paleontologist Dr. Alan Grant, paleobotanist Dr. Ellie Sattler, chaotician and mathematician Dr. Ian Malcolm, and his own young grandchildren, Lex and Tim, to take a preview tour of the exotic resort. Wikipedia The excitement of seeing living dinosaurs quickly turns to terror when a severe tropical storm strikes the island. Concurrently, the park's disgruntled chief computer programmer, Dennis Nedry, commits corporate espionage by shutting down the island's main security grids and electric fences to steal frozen dinosaur embryos for a corporate rival. This total power failure stalls the tour vehicles directly in front of the Tyrannosaurus rex enclosure. Deprived of electricity, the containment fences fail, and the predatory T. rex breaks out, attacking the vehicles, injuring Malcolm, and scattering the remaining survivors across the dangerous terrain of the island. Wikipedia As the storm rages, the survivors must navigate the wilderness to escape the apex predators now roaming free. While Dr. Grant protects the children and helps them trek back across the park, Dr. Sattler and the park's remaining staff attempt to reboot the master computer systems to restore the power and security fences. However, shutting down the power accidentally releases the highly intelligent and lethal Velociraptors, who systematically hunt the characters through the maintenance facilities and visitor center. Ultimately, cornered by the raptors, the surviving humans are inadvertently saved when the T. rex arrives and kills the smaller predators, allowing Hammond, Grant, Sattler, Malcolm, and the children to narrowly escape the island via helicopter.
